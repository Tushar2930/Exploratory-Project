Welcome to Brain Tumour Detection Model.

How the Model Works?

We have created a model that detects whether a patient is suffering from Brain Tumour by analyzing the MRI scan of the patient uploaded by the user. The model uses different deep learning models for brain tumour detection. The code uses the Keras and TensorFlow libraries in Python for building and training the models.

Timeline

1879

First successful brain tumor removal surgery

Scottish surgeon William Macewen performs the first successful brain tumor removal in a young woman.

1970s

CT scans and MRI arrive

British company EMI develops CT scanner that provides pictures of patients brains to be seen for the first time.Researchers at EMI Laboratories obtain the first Magnetic resonance imaging (MR) of a human brain.

1990s

Advancement and Standardisation

Advances in chemotherapy and radiation increases survival for patients. World Health Organization develops universal system for classifying brain tumors with aims at improving communication and translate research findings

2010s

New techniques come to existence

Today, cancer immunotherapy is being actively studied. Primary brain tumors occur in around 250,000 people a year globally, making up less than 2% of cancers. Figures for incidence on brain cancer show predominance in more developed countries.

Precautions

Get proper sleep

Getting a good night’s sleep is incredibly important for your health. In fact, it’s just as important as eating a balanced, nutritious diet and exercising.

Quit smoking

When you quit smoking, your immune system is no longer exposed to tar and nicotine. It will become stronger, and you will be less likely to get sick.

Avioid exposure to radiation

Radiation can damage the DNA in our cells. High doses of radiation can cause Acute Radiation Syndrome (ARS) or Cutaneous Radiation Injuries (CRI).

Exercise regularly

Exercise stimulates your body to release proteins and other chemicals that improve the structure and function of your brain. It also keeps your body fit and healthy.

Eat healthy

A healthy diet typically includes foods from all of the major food groups, including fruits and vegetables of many colors.It includes eating nutritious foods.

Avoid Exposure to carcinogenic chemicals

A few well-known carcinogens are asbestos, nickel, cadmium, radon, vinyl chloride, benzidene, and benzene.
