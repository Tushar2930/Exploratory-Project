How the Model Works?

We have created a model that detects whether a patient is infected with CoVID-19 by analyzing the CT scan of the patient uploaded by the user. The model is an application of deep learning and uses CNN for detection. Our goal is to minimize the time required for administrative work and data analysis by healthcare experts.

Timeline

2019

First Covid-19 Case reported in Wuhan, China

Some pateints develop symptoms of SARS-COV-2. Within no time, Covid spreads to the whole of China. International flights suspended to prevent further spread.

2020

Covid creates havoc worldwide

Due to its high Rh factor, Covid-19 spreads to a large population in China causing international flights to be suspended. Lockdowns announced in many countries with a scary rise in cases. 10 million cases reported by June 2020 and 80 million by year end. On the positive side, 212 Vaccines were under development worldwide.

2021

Vaccines come to humanity's rescue

Pfizer and Moderna launch the first vaccines for Covid-19 at the start of year. Indian giants like Serum Institute and Bharat Biotech also follow and set up massive manufacturing units. World's largest vaccine drive starts in India with a 1.2B population to be vaccinated with two doses.

2022

Humanity wins, Covid loses

With reformed habits and vaccine shots, we win our fight against the pandemic as things return to normalcy. With travel and in-person meetings slowly picking up pace, world starts a new way of life.

Precautions

Wash hands on regular intervals

Handwashing with soap removes germs from hands. This helps prevent infections because: People frequently touch their eyes, nose, and mouth without even realizing it.

Wear Masks

It is most important to choose one that you can wear correctly, that fits closely to your face over your mouth and nose, that provides good protection, and that is comfortable for you.

Get your protection

COVID-19 vaccination helps protect you by creating an antibody response without you having to experience potentially severe illness or post-COVID conditions.

Avoid Crowd

Social distancing slows and decreases transmission of Covid-19 which limits the effects on individuals plus schools, workplaces and the health care system.

Eat healthy

A healthy diet typically includes foods from all of the major food groups, including fruits and vegetables of many colors.It includes eating nutritious foods.

Be clean

Cleaning and disinfecting high-touch surfaces in your home regularly is an important precaution to lower the risk of infection.
